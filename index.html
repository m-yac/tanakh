Visual Tanakh

This is a visualization of which portions of the Tanakh (Hebrew bible) are read in traditional Ashkenazi Jewish services. Each box represents a chapter, sized according to its number of verses. Regions colored yellow to magenta correspond to weekly Shabbat morning readings, and regions colored purple correspond to readings for holidays, festivals, or special Shabbats. Hovering over a spot on the diagram displays info about which chapter and verse it corresponds to and when it is read (click to lock this info on screen). All data is from Hebcal.
